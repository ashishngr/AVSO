Skin Treatments

Acne Management

Ideal for: Whiteheads, blackheads, inflammatory acne, maskne, and early acne scars.

How it works: Tailored plan combining topical/oral medical therapy with procedures like comedone extraction, chemical peels, and microneedling for scar remodeling.

  • Session time20–60 minutes
  • DowntimeLow (redness/peel 1–3 days)
  • ResultsClearer skin in 6–12 weeks with maintenance

Pre-care: Pause retinoids 3 days prior, avoid active acne picking.

Post-care: Gentle cleanser, SPF 50+, avoid gym/steam for 24 hours after procedures.

Note: Prescription medicines are provided only after clinical evaluation.

Pigmentation Correction

Ideal for: Melasma, tanning, post-inflammatory marks.

How it works: Protocols may include sunscreen strategy, brightening topicals, peels, and laser toning depending on type & depth.

  • Session time15–40 minutes
  • DowntimeMinimal to moderate depending on peel
  • ResultsGradual brightening across 4–8 sessions

Pre-care: Strict sun protection 2 weeks before.

Post-care: Hydration, SPF, avoid heat exposure for 48 hours.

Chemical Peels

Ideal for: Dullness, uneven tone, fine lines, acne.

How it works: Safe, controlled exfoliation using AHA/BHA/TCA formulations to stimulate renewal.

  • Session time20–30 minutes
  • DowntimeNone to 3 days (light flaking)
  • ResultsBrighter, smoother skin in 1–2 weeks

Pre-care: Avoid waxing/actives 3 days before.

Post-care: SPF, gentle moisturiser; no scrubs for 1 week.

Microneedling (with/without PRP)

Ideal for: Acne scars, enlarged pores, texture, early lines.

How it works: Creates micro-channels to trigger collagen remodeling; PRP can enhance recovery and outcomes.

  • Session time45–60 minutes
  • DowntimeRedness 24–48 hours
  • ResultsTexture improvement over 3–6 sessions

Pre-care: Antiviral prophylaxis if history of cold sores (as advised).

Post-care: Avoid makeup 24 hours; SPF and barrier repair.

Hair Treatments

PRP for Hair

Ideal for: Early androgenetic alopecia, postpartum thinning, diffuse hair weakness.

How it works: Concentrated platelets from your own blood are injected into the scalp to support follicle health.

  • Session time45–60 minutes
  • DowntimeNone (mild soreness 1–2 days)
  • ResultsImproved density over 3–6 sessions

Pre-care: Hydrate well; avoid blood thinners unless advised by your physician.

Post-care: No hair oiling or sauna for 24 hours; gentle shampoo next day.

Hair Transplant (FUE)

Ideal for: Receding hairline, crown thinning with stable donor.

How it works: Follicular Unit Extraction moves resistant follicles to thinning zones with natural hairline design.

  • Session time4–8 hours (day care)
  • Downtime5–7 days (crusting/shedding)
  • ResultsNew growth visible at 3–4 months; full result by 12 months

Pre-care: Stop smoking 2 weeks prior; medical clearance if required.

Post-care: Saline sprays, gentle wash; avoid strenuous activity 10–14 days.

Scalp Health Program

Ideal for: Dandruff, seborrheic dermatitis, itchy or oily scalp.

How it works: Targeted antifungal/anti-inflammatory care with in-clinic exfoliation and home routine.

  • Session time30–40 minutes
  • DowntimeNone
  • ResultsRelief within 2–4 weeks with maintenance

Laser Treatments

Laser Hair Reduction

Ideal for: Unwanted facial/body hair.

How it works: Laser light targets hair follicles to reduce growth safely and effectively over a series of sessions.

  • Session time15–60 minutes (area dependent)
  • DowntimeNone
  • ResultsLong-term reduction after 6–8 sessions

Pre-care: Shave 24 hours prior; avoid waxing/threading 4 weeks before.

Post-care: SPF and soothing gel; avoid sun/sauna 48 hours.

Laser Toning

Ideal for: Tan, uneven tone, mild pigmentation.

How it works: Low-fluence laser breaks up pigment and boosts radiance with minimal downtime.

  • Session time20–30 minutes
  • DowntimeNone to mild pinkness
  • ResultsInstant glow; cumulative clarity over 4–6 sessions

Minor Lesion Removal

Ideal for: Skin tags, warts, benign growths (after evaluation).

How it works: Quick in-clinic procedure with local anaesthesia and aftercare guidance.

  • Session time20–30 minutes
  • DowntimeScab for 5–7 days
  • ResultsImmediate removal; healing in 1–2 weeks

Cosmetology

Medical Facial (Hydra-derma)

Ideal for: Dull, congested skin pre-event refresh.

How it works: Cleanse, exfoliate, extract, hydrate, and infuse—with device-assisted steps tailored to your skin type.

  • Session time45–60 minutes
  • DowntimeNone
  • ResultsInstant glow and smoothness for 2–4 weeks

Anti‑Aging Injectables

Ideal for: Dynamic wrinkles, volume loss, facial contour.

How it works: Neuromodulators soften expression lines; dermal fillers restore youthful volume and shape.

  • Session time30–60 minutes
  • DowntimeMinimal (bruising/swelling)
  • ResultsVisible in days; lasts 4–12 months (product dependent)

Performed after medical assessment; pregnancy/breastfeeding are contraindications.

Pricing and plans are customized after consultation. Package discounts available for multi-session treatments.
