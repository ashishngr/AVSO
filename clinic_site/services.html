Skin Treatments

Acne Scars

Skin

Ideal for: Atrophic scars (ice‑pick, boxcar, rolling), post‑acne marks.

How it works: Personalised combinations of subcision, TCA CROSS, microneedling (Dermapen 4), PRP, and targeted peels to remodel collagen.

  • Session time30–60 minutes
  • Downtime1–3 days redness/flaking
  • ResultsGradual smoothing over 3–6 sessions

Pre-care: Pause retinoids 3–5 days prior; antiviral prophylaxis if indicated.

Post-care: SPF 50+, barrier repair, avoid heat/steam 48 hours.

Benefits:

  • Improves depth and visibility of scars safely
  • Custom plan based on scar type and skin tone
  • Combines modalities for faster, better outcomes

Pigmentation Correction

Skin

Ideal for: Melasma, tanning, post‑inflammatory hyperpigmentation.

How it works: Brightening topicals, sunscreen strategy, peels and laser toning calibrated to pigment type and depth.

  • Session time15–40 minutes
  • DowntimeMinimal to moderate (peel dependent)
  • ResultsGradual clarity over 4–8 sessions

Pre-care: Strict photoprotection 2 weeks prior.

Post-care: Hydration, SPF, avoid heat exposure 48 hours.

Benefits:

  • Evens tone and reduces blotchiness
  • Protocols adapted to melasma vs tanning
  • Focus on safe, gradual brightening

Chemical Peels

Skin

  • Targets acne, dullness and fine lines

Hair Treatments

Hair Transplant (FUE)

Hair

Ideal for: Receding hairline, crown thinning with stable donor.

How it works: Follicular Unit Extraction moves resistant follicles to thinning zones with natural hairline design.

  • Session time4–8 hours (day care)
  • Downtime5–7 days (crusting/shedding)
  • ResultsNew growth 3–4 months; full by 12 months

Pre-care: Stop smoking 2 weeks prior; medical clearance if required.

Post-care: Saline sprays, gentle wash; avoid strenuous activity 10–14 days.

Benefits:

  • Permanent redistribution of your own hair
  • Natural hairline artistry
  • Day‑care, local anaesthesia procedure

Hair PRP / GFC

Hair

Ideal for: Early androgenetic alopecia, postpartum thinning, diffuse weakness.

How it works: Platelet‑derived growth factors (PRP/GFC) are injected to support follicle health and hair cycle.

  • Session time45–60 minutes
  • DowntimeNone (mild soreness 1–2 days)
  • ResultsThicker feel over 3–6 sessions

Pre-care: Hydrate well; avoid blood thinners unless advised.

Post-care: No hair oiling/sauna for 24 hours; gentle shampoo next day.

Benefits:

  • Autologous growth factors, drug‑free
  • Supports miniaturised follicles
  • Complements medications and lifestyle
